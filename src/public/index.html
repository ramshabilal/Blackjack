How to Play:

1. Enter the start values for the hand in the input field (optional).

2. Click "Play" to start the game.

3. The game will simulate one hand of Blackjack based on your input.

Detailed Instructions:

- The start values should include numbers or a description that simulates a real Blackjack hand.

- After clicking "Play," the game will deal cards and automatically handle scoring based on Blackjack rules.

- Scoring is based on the standard Blackjack rules: Aces can count as 1 or 11, face cards (King, Queen, Jack) are worth 10 points, and all other cards are worth their face value.

- The objective is to have a hand value closest to 21 without going over (bust).

- After the hand is completed, the result will display whether you won, lost, or pushed (tie).
